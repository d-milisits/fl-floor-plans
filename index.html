FL Floor Plans was founded to fill the need for an unbiased measuring company. Those in the industry know companies will often inflate square footage numbers to add extra profit margin to a job. With few alternatives available, developers, contractors, realtors and home owners are forced to use biased services to measure properties, ultimately being overcharged for projects.

FL Floor Plans was created with customer needs in mind, as we don't party with any flooring installation service or flooring providers. We began our measuring service in 2016 and measure everything single room jobs to entire properties. Have peace of mind knowing you're receiving the best measuring service available to you.
